About Stony Bend Barn

Our Story

At Stony Bend Barn, we are driven by a passion for crafting timeless, beautiful, and functional pieces built to last for generations. What began as a personal endeavor—creating custom designs for friends and family—has evolved into a continued dedication to the art of woodworking.

Our Process

We begin with meticulously chosen wood and craft each piece with precision and care. Attention to detail and quality craftsmanship are paramount, whether we’re creating a cutting board or a set of coasters. Every step of the process is carefully considered to meet the highest standards of excellence.

Our Services

We offer a variety of woodworking services, including custom engraving with CNC and laser technology. Our commitment to exceptional service ensures every project is tailored to meet your needs. Reach out today to discuss your next woodworking project—we’ll help bring your ideas to life.
